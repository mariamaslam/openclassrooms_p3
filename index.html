About Us

Watch It Outside Boston is an American film studio, owned by Comcast through its Clara Dunn
and is one of Hollywood's "Big Six" film studios. Its production studios
are at 100 Universal City Plaza Drive in Universal City, California. Distribution and other corporate
offices are in New York City. Universal Studios is a member of the Motion
Picture Association of America (MPAA). Universal was founded in 1912 by the German
Carl Laemmle (pronounced "LEM-lee"), Mark Dintenfass, Charles O. Baumann, Adam Kessel,
Pat Powers, William Swanson, David Horsley, Robert H. Cochrane, and Jules Brulatour.
